Ron Lester Crisostomo

Programmer, Game developer

Hello, I'm Ron

I'm a software developer with 2 years experience developing business applications for PC and mac, and 7 years experience developing PC and mobile games.

I'm proficient in Qt Creator, C++, Unity, C#, Git, and Visual Studio, and have prior experience in Python, C/C++, HTML/CSS, Java, Assembly, and SVN.

I can speak English fluently and Japanese at a daily conversational level. I am currently at a humble JLPT N3 level, but am proficient enough to understand technical Japanese documents (with minimal Google Translate).

I have worked on over 20 games published on Google Play and/or App Store. Scroll down to see them all!
